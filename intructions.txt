This game is a ZORK style text-based adventure game called ‘Dungeon Escape’ for Dewey: The DATA cabinet. In ‘Dungeon Escape’, you play as a wrongfully accused prisoner, and work to escape your confines. Puzzles will need to be solved by taking items throughout the world and experimenting to find solutions. Beyond simple commands, the commands of the game are left a secret to be uncovered.
To properly experience the game, make sure to read the info text at the start, and use the ‘HELP’ command to get the basic commands and command syntax. Additionally, the scrappy note will provide goals, and give you information necessary to get out of the starting cell.
